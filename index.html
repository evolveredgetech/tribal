About Tribal App

Tribal App is a digital platform to preserve and celebrate the language, culture, and traditions of tribal communities. It connects new generations with their heritage in an engaging and modern way.

What We Offer

Language Learning

Learn native tribal languages through interactive tools, pronunciation practice, and quizzes to grow your cultural knowledge.

Culture & Traditions

Discover songs, folk stories, rituals, and customs, carefully curated by elders and researchers.

Festival Updates

Get information about tribal festivals, celebrations, and gatherings happening near you.

Our Vision

To create a future where tribal communities are empowered, culturally enriched, and connected—preserving languages and traditions for generations.

Our Mission

We aim to preserve and promote tribal heritage digitally, supporting language, culture, and the upliftment of tribal communities.

Content Collection & Cultural Preservation

Based on oral traditions, all data is respectfully collected and stored to protect and educate about tribal heritage and knowledge.
